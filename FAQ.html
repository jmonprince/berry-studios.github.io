FAQ

Q: DOES BERRY STUDIOS RESTOCK OLDER ITEMS?

A: NO, WE DO NOT RESTOCK. ITEMS ARE TYPICALLY AVAILABLE ON OUR WEBSTORE FOR AT LEAST 2 WEEKS, THEN THE STORE IS CLOSED IN PREPARATION FOR THE NEXT RELEASE.

Q: HOW LONG DO ORDERS TAKE TO SHIP?

A: ITEMS PURCHASED THROUGH OUR WEBSTORE TAKE AT LEAST 2 WEEKS TO BE PROCESSED AND SHIPPED.

Q: WHEN IS THE NEXT DROP?

A: WE STRIVE TO RELEASE CONSISTENTLY BUT ADVISE SUPPOERTERS TO FOLLOW US ON SOCIALS TO STAY UP TO DATE.

Q: WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

A: ALL SALES ARE FINAL. NO EXCHANGES OR REFUNDS.

Q: HOW CAN I MODEL/DESIGN/SHOOT FOR BERRY STUDIOS?

A: WE GENERALLY CONTACT INDIVIDUALS WE HAVE IN MIND BUT WE DO ACCEPT RESUMES AND PORTFOLIOS @ BERRYSTUDIOS.INC@GMAIL.COM
