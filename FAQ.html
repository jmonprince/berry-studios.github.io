FAQ (COVID-19 UPDATES)

Q: DOES BERRY STUDIOS RESTOCK OLDER ITEMS?

A: NO, WE TYPICALLY DO NOT RESTOCK. HOWEVER, SINCE OUR UPCOMING SEASON HAS BEEN DELAYED, WE HAVE PUT SOME OF OUR OLDER ITEMS ON THE STORE FOR THE TIME BEING.

Q: HOW LONG DO ORDERS TAKE TO SHIP?

A: WE HAVE BEEN WORKING CLOSELY WITH OUR MANUFACTURERS TO ENSURE ALL ORDERS ARE GOING OUT AS FAST AS POSSIBLE UNDER CURRENT CIRCUMSTANCES. DUE TO THE VIRUS, SOME ITEMS MAY EXPERIENCE DELAYS IN SHIPPING. SHIPPING ESTIMATES CURRENTLY LAND BETWEEN 4-6 WEEKS FOR NORMAL ITEMS AND UP TO 6+ WEEKS FOR PRE-ORDER ITEMS.

Q: HOW DOES SHIPPING WORK?

A: BERRY STUDIOS IMPORTS ITS GOODS FROM SEVERAL SOURCES AROUND THE WORLD. CURRENTLY A MAJORITY OF THE ITEMS ON OUR SITE ARE DROPSHIPPED STRAIGHT FROM THE FACTORY TO THE CUSTOMER SO I (JMON PRINCE) HAVE NO CONTROL OVER SHIPPING FOR SOME ORDERS. IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS ABOUT YOUR SPECIFIC ORDER PLEASE EMAIL BERRYSTUDIOS.INC@GMAIL.COM.

Q: WHEN IS THE NEXT DROP?

A: WE STRIVE TO RELEASE CONSISTENTLY BUT ADVISE SUPPORTERS TO FOLLOW US ON SOCIALS TO STAY UP TO DATE.

Q: WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

A: ALL SALES ARE FINAL. NO EXCHANGES OR REFUNDS.

Q: HOW CAN I MODEL/DESIGN/SHOOT FOR BERRY STUDIOS?

A: WE GENERALLY CONTACT INDIVIDUALS WE HAVE IN MIND BUT WE DO ACCEPT RESUMES AND PORTFOLIOS @ BERRYSTUDIOS.INC@GMAIL.COM
